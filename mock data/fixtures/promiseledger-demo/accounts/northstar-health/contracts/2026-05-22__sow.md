# Statement of Work

## Services

Vendor will configure regulated workflow routing, role-based queues, and admin audit log views for Northstar Health.

## Deliverables

Vendor will provide approved security materials by May 22, 2026. Vendor will provide an implementation kickoff plan after security approval, signed BAA, and procurement PO are complete.

## Timeline

Kickoff occurs within 15 business days after security approval, signed BAA, and procurement PO are complete. No onboarding activity that processes patient-adjacent workflow data may begin before those dependencies are complete.

## Customer Obligations

Northstar must provide procurement portal access, legal redlines, a signed BAA, and procurement PO before kickoff is scheduled.

## Vendor Obligations

Vendor must maintain admin audit logs for configured workflow actions during the deployment term.

## Exclusions

BAA language remains subject to final Legal review. Immutable audit exports and custom HIPAA reports are excluded unless added through change control.

## SLAs / Support Terms

Implementation support is provided during business hours. The SOW does not guarantee kickoff during the week of May 25, 2026.

## Change Control

Any change to BAA language, audit export scope, or kickoff criteria must be documented in a signed change order.
