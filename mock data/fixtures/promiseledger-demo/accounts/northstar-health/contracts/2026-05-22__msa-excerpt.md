# Master Services Agreement Excerpt

## Services

Vendor will provide subscription services and implementation services described in the applicable SOW.

## Deliverables

Security documentation may be provided as standard policy documents unless an SOW specifies custom deliverables.

## Timeline

Implementation dates are conditioned on completion of legal, security, and procurement prerequisites.

## Customer Obligations

Customer is responsible for timely redlines, procurement documentation, and system access.

## Vendor Obligations

Vendor will not process patient-adjacent workflow data before a BAA is mutually executed.

## Exclusions

BAA terms are not effective until both parties execute the BAA. No PHI processing is authorized before BAA execution.

## SLAs / Support Terms

Standard support applies unless an addendum states otherwise.

## Change Control

Material legal terms require written amendment or signed addendum.
