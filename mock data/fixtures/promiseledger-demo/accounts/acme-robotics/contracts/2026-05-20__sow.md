# Statement of Work

## Services

Vendor will configure a controlled AI workflow pilot for renewal account triage, approval queues, and preview-only outbound communications.

## Deliverables

Vendor will provide the security packet by May 22, 2026. Vendor will provide a renewal dashboard preview plan by May 27, 2026.

## Timeline

Kickoff may occur no earlier than May 28, 2026 and only after Acme provides the procurement checklist, field map, sandbox credentials, and admin approver.

## Customer Obligations

Acme must provide the NetSuite field map, sandbox credentials, procurement checklist, and designated pilot admin before kickoff.

## Vendor Obligations

Vendor must keep external email delivery in preview-only mode during the pilot unless the parties sign a separate go-live approval.

## Exclusions

NetSuite integration is excluded from this SOW. Any NetSuite connector requires separate Product approval and a signed change order. Live external email sending is excluded from the pilot.

## SLAs / Support Terms

The pilot includes business-hours support for setup questions. No production automation SLA applies.

## Change Control

Any integration work outside the listed deliverables requires written change control signed by both parties.
