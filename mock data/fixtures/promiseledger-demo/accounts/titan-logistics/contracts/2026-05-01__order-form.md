# Order Form

## Services

Vendor will provide routing visibility software, dispatch exception workflows, and standard support access for Titan Logistics.

## Deliverables

- Access to routing dashboard features for configured fleets.
- Standard support queue access.
- Monthly account review during the renewal transition period.

## Timeline

The current order form is effective May 1, 2026 through August 31, 2026. Renewal review materials are targeted before June 30, 2026.

## Customer Obligations

Titan must provide complete route IDs, screenshots, timestamps, and fleet region details for reported issues.

## Vendor Obligations

Vendor will acknowledge urgent support cases within four business hours during business days.

## Exclusions

The order form does not guarantee full resolution by a specific date. New dashboard functionality, including carrier ETA variance enhancements, requires separate written approval.

## SLAs / Support Terms

Support targets are commercially reasonable efforts and are not guaranteed resolution times. Urgent case acknowledgement is targeted within four business hours.

## Change Control

Any change to support terms, guaranteed resolution times, or new dashboard functionality must be documented in a signed amendment.
