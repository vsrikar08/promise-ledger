# Proposal

## Scope

Pilot deliverables are limited to simulation runs and operator-approved recommendations. No production grid control-plane action is included.

## Timeline

Kickoff and simulation workspace setup begin on 2026-05-27. Operator approval queue ready for the 2026-05-29 tabletop exercise. Audit log export available by 2026-06-03.

## Included

- Replayed grid-event simulation workspace.
- Operator approval queue for recommendations.
- Audit log export for operator decisions.
- Safety review packet delivery by 2026-05-20.

## Not Included / Assumptions

Automatic dispatch to live grid systems is not included in this proposal. Any future live automation path requires separate Safety, Product, and legal approval.

## Customer Responsibilities

HelioGrid will provide redacted dispatch rules and two sample event logs by 2026-05-22. HelioGrid will nominate two operators for the tabletop exercise by 2026-05-26.

## Pricing

Pilot services are priced at 240000 USD for the regulated infrastructure pilot term.
