# Statement of Work

## Services

PromiseLedger will provide a regulated infrastructure pilot for replayed grid-event simulation, recommendation routing, and operator approval workflows. The pilot is simulation-only until joint safety review approval is complete.

## Deliverables

- Simulation workspace configured for HelioGrid event replay.
- Operator approval queue for recommendations.
- Audit log export for operator decision records.

## Timeline

Simulation workspace kickoff is scheduled for 2026-05-27. Operator approval workflow review is scheduled for 2026-05-29. Safety review approval checkpoint is scheduled for 2026-06-06.

## Customer Obligations

HelioGrid must provide redacted operating rules by 2026-05-22. HelioGrid must provide two sample event logs by 2026-05-22.

## Vendor Obligations

PromiseLedger must maintain auditable records of simulated recommendations and operator approvals during the pilot.

## Exclusions

No live control-plane actions, automatic dispatch, or grid-affecting external actions are authorized under this SOW.

## SLAs / Support Terms

Pilot support is provided during business hours with critical safety questions escalated to Product and Security within one business day.

## Data Processing / Privacy Terms

Customer event logs must be redacted before upload to the pilot workspace. No production grid credentials will be stored.

## Change Control

Any move from simulation-only pilot activity to live automation requires a written amendment signed by both parties.
